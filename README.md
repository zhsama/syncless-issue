## What is Syncless?

Syncless is an **agent that works across your world** — your browsers, MacBooks, servers, clusters, and even your teammates.

Its vision is simple: **stop being the middleman**. Don't let AI write for AI to read. Don't let humans become appendages of AI.

With Syncless, you can `@` any of your devices and let them talk to each other. You can even `@` your colleagues or friends and ask them to help — all orchestrated by the agent, seamlessly.

## Key Features

- **🖥️ Multi-Device Agent** — `@` your browser, MacBook, server, or any machine. The agent works directly on the device.
- **🔗 Device Interconnection** — Your devices talk to each other. Move context, files, and actions across machines effortlessly.
- **👥 Human-in-the-Loop Collaboration** — `@` your teammates. Delegate work to real people when AI isn't enough, without losing context.
- **🚫 No More Copy-Paste** — The agent reads, writes, and acts where the work actually happens. You stay in control without being the relay.

## Syncless 是什么？

Syncless 是一个**跨越你整个世界运作的 Agent** —— 你的浏览器、MacBook、服务器、集群，甚至你的同事。

它的愿景很简单：**不再让人成为传话筒**。不要 AI 写文 AI 读，不要让人成为 AI 的附庸。

在 Syncless 中，你可以 `@` 任意一台设备，让它们互联互通。你甚至可以 `@` 你的同事、朋友，让他们帮你做一些事情 —— 一切由 Agent 无缝串联。

## 核心功能

- **🖥️ 多设备 Agent** — `@` 你的浏览器、MacBook、服务器或任意机器。Agent 直接在设备上工作。
- **🔗 设备互联** — 你的设备之间可以互相通信，轻松跨机器移动上下文、文件和操作。
- **👥 人机协作** — `@` 你的团队成员。当 AI 力不从心时，将工作委派给真人，而不丢失上下文。
- **🚫 告别复制粘贴** — Agent 在工作实际发生的地方读、写、执行。你保持掌控，不再做中转站。
